StatBomb
Dublin, Ireland · Founded 2015
Private
The gold standard for event data collection. StatBomb tracks over 3,000 data points per match including on-ball pressure, pass foot, and shot freeze frames. Their xG model is widely considered the most sophisticated in the industry.
Funding: €12M Series A
Clients: 100+ clubs
Coverage: 20+ leagues
Key clients: Manchester City, FC Barcelona, Bayer Leverkusen, Bundesliga, Serie A, Belgian Pro League

Hudl / Wyscout
Lincoln, NE · Founded 2006
Private
The dominant video analysis platform across all sports. Hudl acquired Wyscout in 2019 for €30M, creating the largest football video and data marketplace. Their platform serves 200,000+ teams globally with tools for recruitment, opposition analysis, and player development.
Users: 200K+ teams
Videos: 500K+ matches
Valuation: $800M+ est.
Subsidiaries: Wyscout, VolleyMetrics, Sportscode. Partners: 30+ sports including football, basketball, American football

Second Spectrum
Los Angeles, CA · Founded 2013
Acquired
Pioneers in AI-driven player tracking using computer vision. Their optical tracking system captures player and ball movement without wearable hardware. Acquired by Genius Sports in 2021 for $200M, now powers tracking for the Premier League, NBA, and MLS.
Acquired: $200M (2021)
Parent: Genius Sports
Leagues: PL, NBA, MLS
Now integrated into Genius Sports' betting and broadcast products. Provides real-time tracking data to rights holders.

Catapult Sports
Melbourne, Australia · Founded 2006
Public
The leader in wearable athlete tracking. Catapult's GPS vests and Vector devices monitor player load, sprint distance, and injury risk. IPO'd on the ASX in 2014. Serves 3,200+ elite teams including Real Madrid, Bayern Munich, and the NFL.
Ticker: CAT · ASX
Teams: 3,200+
Market cap: A$250M
Products: Vector GPS, ClearSky LPS, Thundercloud video. Acquired XOS Technologies and SBG Sports.

SciSports
Amersfoort, Netherlands · Founded 2012
Private
Dutch analytics firm specialising in player scouting and transfer recommendations. Their SciSkill algorithm evaluates 60,000+ players across 200+ leagues. Known for identifying undervalued talent in secondary markets before major clubs move.
Players: 60K+ tracked
Leagues: 200+
Funding: €5M+ raised
Clients include AZ Alkmaar, Feyenoord, and multiple Championship clubs. Partnership with KPMG for due diligence.

Track160
Tel Aviv, Israel · Founded 2017
Startup
Israeli startup using single-camera AI tracking for grassroots and semi-pro clubs. Their system requires no hardware installation — just a standard camera feed. Recently signed deals with the Israeli Premier League and Belgian lower divisions.
Cameras: 1 needed
Cost: 90% cheaper
